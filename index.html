Welcome to personal webpage

I am a mathematician working in functional analysis and operator theory. I did my PhD from the School of Physical Sciences, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. Currently I am serving as a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Mathematics, Physics and Mechanics, Ljubljana (Slovenia). In the past I have also served as postdoctoral fellow at IISER-Bhopal and IIT-Gandhinagar.

I am primarily interested in Banach/Topological and Operator algebras. I study classification properties like Arens regularity and Amenability of Banach algebras. Some of the objects that I study are \(C^*\)-algebras, locally \(C^*\)-algebras, Groups and Fourier algebras of a locally compact group and their biduals. To study these objects in the context of Arens regularity/Amenability, I often deal with the various tensor products (like projective, injective, operator space tensor products etc.) of these algebras, tools from geometry of Banach spaces (like RNP, WSC etc) and techniques of topological vector spaces (like projective/injective limits, various dual topologies and theory of locally convex vector spaces).
